Our Story

Meet the family behind The Omo Eaters

From Our Kitchen to Your Heart

The Omo Eaters was born from a simple dream: to share the rich, comforting flavors of Nigerian home cooking with everyone. What started as small family dinners has grown into a beloved food destination.

Every recipe we serve has been passed down through generations, perfected in our family kitchen, and made with the same love we give our own children. "Omo" means child in Yoruba, and we treat every customer like family.

We believe that good food brings people together, and we're honored to be part of your family's mealtime memories.
